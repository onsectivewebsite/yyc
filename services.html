Our Services

Helping newcomers, students, and families find the right housing in Calgary.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Housing Support

From initial consultation to settling in, our integrated approach ensures you find the right home at every stage.

01

Housing Search Assistance

Helping newcomers, students, and families find rental and purchase options that fit their needs and budget.

We work closely with you to understand your housing priorities and match you with suitable properties across Calgary. Whether you're looking to rent or buy, our team searches available listings, arranges viewings, and helps you navigate the local market with confidence.

Personalized Housing Matching

Tailored searches based on your needs, lifestyle, and budget

Budget-Friendly Options

Finding quality housing that fits within your financial means

02

Application & Lease Support

Guiding you through rental applications, lease agreements, and tenant rights so you feel informed and prepared.

Navigating rental applications and lease documents can be overwhelming, especially if you're new to Canada. We walk you through every step, from completing applications and gathering references to understanding your lease terms and knowing your rights as a tenant in Alberta.

Application Guidance

Step-by-step help with rental applications and documentation

Tenant Rights Education

Understanding your rights and responsibilities as a renter in Alberta

03

Affordable Housing Guidance

Connecting you with affordable housing programs, subsidies, and government resources to ease your housing journey.

We help you identify and access affordable housing programs, rental subsidies, and government-funded resources available in Calgary and Alberta. Our team stays up to date on eligibility criteria and application processes so we can connect you with the support you qualify for.

Subsidy Program Navigation

Helping you find and apply for housing subsidies and financial aid

Community Housing Connections

Linking you with community organizations and housing providers

04

Neighborhood Orientation

Helping you understand Calgary neighborhoods, schools, transit, and amenities so you can choose the right area.

Choosing where to live is about more than the home itself. We help you explore Calgary's diverse neighborhoods, understand school options, learn transit routes, and discover nearby amenities so you can make an informed decision about where to settle your family.

Area Walkthroughs

Guided tours and insights into Calgary's neighborhoods

Community Resource Mapping

Identifying schools, transit, and essential services near your home

Our Process

How We Work

A proven four-step approach that guides you from first conversation to settled home.

01

Consultation

We begin by understanding your situation, housing needs, and preferences through an in-depth conversation.

02

Assessment

Our team evaluates your budget, eligibility for programs, and identifies the best housing options for you.

03

Housing Search

We search available listings, arrange viewings, and help you navigate applications and lease agreements.

04

Settlement Support

Once you've found your home, we help you settle in with neighborhood orientation and ongoing guidance.

Need Help Finding a Home?

Whether you're new to Calgary or looking for a better place to call home, we're here to help you every step of the way.
