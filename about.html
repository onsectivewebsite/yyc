Our Mission

Empowering Calgary Through Housing Access

Foresight YYC Inc. was founded on a simple yet powerful premise: everyone who arrives in Calgary deserves help finding a safe, dignified place to call home.

We help newcomers, international students, refugees, and families navigate Calgary's housing market. Through our partnerships with construction firms, legal services, and property management companies, we connect people with the housing options that best fit their needs and budgets. We do not sell or list properties — we guide and support people through the process of finding a home.

Our team works directly with individuals and families, providing hands-on assistance with housing searches, applications, and lease processes. Every person we help is one step closer to building a stable life in Calgary.
