Our Commitment

A Housing-Focused Social Mission

At Foresight YYC, we are dedicated to helping newcomers, international students, refugees, and families find safe, quality housing across Calgary.

Through our partnerships with refugee-supporting not-for-profits and affordable housing organizations, we work to ensure that Calgary's most vulnerable populations have access to dignified housing. We guide newcomers through the complexities of the Canadian rental and housing market, connect them with suitable neighborhoods, and support them through every step of the process.

Our work goes beyond simply finding a place to live. We help families understand their housing rights, navigate lease agreements, and integrate into their new communities so they can build a stable foundation for their future in Calgary.

Our Initiatives

Programs That Make a Difference

Dedicated programs designed to address Calgary's most pressing housing challenges.

Newcomer Support

Newcomer Housing Support

Helping newcomers to Calgary find safe, comfortable homes by guiding them through housing searches, applications, and the transition into their new communities.

Student Housing

Student Housing Program

Assisting international students in finding affordable, convenient housing near Calgary's campuses, ensuring they can focus on their education in a stable living environment.

Refugee Support

Refugee Housing Assistance

Partnering with not-for-profit organizations to provide housing support for refugees settling in Calgary, from transitional shelter to permanent homes.

Affordability

Affordable Housing Connections

Linking families to affordable housing options across Calgary by connecting them with subsidized programs, below-market rentals, and community housing resources.

Integration

Neighborhood Integration

Helping new residents understand and connect with their communities through neighborhood orientations, local resource guides, and community introductions.

Education

Housing Rights Education

Workshops and resources on tenant rights, lease understanding, and housing regulations, empowering residents to advocate for themselves and make informed decisions.
