Our Commitment

More Than Services — A Social Mission

At Foresight YYC, community impact isn't a side project — it's woven into every decision we make.

Through our partnerships with refugee-supporting not-for-profits, settlement agencies, and community organizations, we work to ensure that Calgary's most vulnerable populations have access to housing, employment, language support, and legal aid. Every family we help settle, every newcomer we welcome, and every community we strengthen represents the true measure of our success.

Our work extends beyond immediate support. We connect families with long-term networks, help them build financial independence, and ensure they have the foundation to create a prosperous future in Calgary.

Our Initiatives

Programs That Make a Difference

Dedicated initiatives designed to address Calgary's most pressing community needs.

Newcomer Support

Refugee & Newcomer Settlement

Comprehensive arrival support including airport pickup, initial housing, documentation assistance, and community orientation for refugees and newcomers.

Housing

Affordable Housing Program

Connecting families with safe, affordable housing across Calgary and providing tenant advocacy and support to prevent homelessness.

Employment

Workforce Integration

Job readiness workshops, resume clinics, employer partnerships, and mentorship programs to help newcomers enter the Canadian workforce.

Education

Language & Literacy

English language classes from beginner to advanced, plus specialized workplace communication workshops and literacy programs.

Integration

Community Connection Events

Cultural exchange events, community dinners, and social gatherings that help newcomers build friendships and feel at home in Calgary.

Youth

Youth Mentorship Program

Supporting newcomer youth with academic tutoring, mentorship, recreational activities, and leadership development opportunities.
