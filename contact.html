FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q

Who can use your services?

Our services are open to everyone — newcomers, international students, refugees, and any Calgarian who needs help finding housing. No matter your background, we're here to assist you.

Q

Are your services free?

Yes. Our core housing assistance services are completely free of charge. We believe everyone deserves access to safe, affordable housing support.

Q

What areas of Calgary do you cover?

We cover all of Calgary and surrounding areas. Whether you're looking in the NE, NW, SE, SW, or nearby communities, our team can help you find housing that fits your needs.

Q

How do you help find housing?

We provide personalized housing search assistance, application and documentation support, lease review and guidance, and neighborhood orientation to help you settle into your new community.

Q

What is your response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent housing matters, please call us directly at 403-430-0050.
