Our Commitment

More Than Real Estate — A Social Mission

At Foresight YYC, community impact isn't a side project — it's woven into every decision we make.

Through our partnerships with refugee-supporting not-for-profits and affordable housing organizations, we work to ensure that Calgary's most vulnerable populations have access to safe, dignified housing. Every family we help settle, every newcomer we welcome, and every community we strengthen represents the true measure of our success.

Our work extends beyond providing shelter. We connect families with support networks, help them navigate the complexities of Canadian housing, and ensure they have the foundation to build a prosperous future in Calgary.

Programs That Make a Difference

Dedicated programs designed to address Calgary's most pressing housing challenges.

Newcomer Support

Refugee & Newcomer Housing Program

Partnering with not-for-profits to provide transitional and permanent housing solutions for refugees and newcomers settling in Calgary.

Affordability

Affordable Housing Initiative

Developing and facilitating the sale of below-market-rate homes, making quality housing accessible to working families across Calgary.
