Our Programs

Comprehensive support services designed to help newcomers and vulnerable community members thrive in Calgary.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Support Programs

From arrival to independence, our integrated approach ensures meaningful support at every stage of the journey.

01

Housing Support

Helping families and individuals find safe, affordable, and dignified housing across Calgary.

We work with landlords, housing agencies, and government programs to connect newcomers and vulnerable populations with suitable housing. Our team assists with applications, lease agreements, tenant rights education, and ongoing advocacy to ensure stable living conditions.

Housing Search Assistance

Personalized support finding suitable, affordable homes

Tenancy Support

Help with applications, leases, and understanding tenant rights

02

Settlement Services

Comprehensive settlement assistance for refugees and newcomers arriving in Calgary.

From airport pickups and initial orientation to healthcare registration and school enrollment, our settlement team guides newcomers through every step. We provide cultural orientation, help navigate government services, and connect families with essential community resources.

Arrival & Orientation

Airport reception, initial housing, and community orientation

Documentation Support

Assistance with SIN, health cards, bank accounts, and more

03

Employment Support

Connecting individuals with meaningful employment opportunities and building career skills for the Canadian job market.

Our employment program includes resume and cover letter workshops, interview preparation, job search strategies, and direct connections with Calgary employers. We also offer skills assessment, credential recognition guidance, and mentorship programs to help newcomers achieve financial independence.

Resume & Interview Prep

Canadian-format resumes and mock interview sessions

Job Placement Support

Connections with local employers and mentorship programs

04

Language & Legal Programs

English language classes and legal assistance to help newcomers navigate life in Canada with confidence.

Our language programs range from beginner to advanced levels, with specialized classes for workplace English and everyday communication. We also provide legal aid referrals, immigration consultation support, and help understanding Canadian laws and rights — empowering individuals to advocate for themselves.

English Language Classes

Beginner to advanced levels, including workplace English

Legal Aid Referrals

Immigration support, tenant rights, and legal navigation

Our Process

How We Help

A proven four-step approach that delivers personalized, meaningful support.

01

Intake & Assessment

We start by listening — understanding your situation, needs, and goals through a confidential intake session.

02

Personalized Plan

Our team creates a tailored support plan, connecting you with the right programs and community resources.

03

Active Support

We walk alongside you through every step — from housing search and job applications to language classes.

04

Ongoing Follow-Up

Our support doesn't stop at placement. We check in regularly and provide continued guidance as you settle in.

Need Support? We're Here for You.

Every journey is unique. Tell us about yours, and we'll connect you with the right programs and resources.
