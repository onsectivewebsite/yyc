Who We Are

A Vision Rooted in Community Impact

Foresight YYC Inc. is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping newcomers, international students, refugees, and families find quality housing across Calgary and surrounding areas.

We partner with construction firms, legal services, property managers, and community organizations to guide individuals through the housing search process — making it simpler, less stressful, and more accessible for everyone.
