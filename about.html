Our Mission

Empowering Calgary Through Compassionate Support

Foresight YYC Inc. was founded on a simple yet powerful premise: every person in Calgary deserves access to the resources they need to build a dignified, stable life.

Over the past decade, we have grown from a focused local initiative into a recognized community support organization in Calgary. Our extensive experience, combined with strong partnerships across government agencies, legal services, healthcare providers, and community organizations, positions us uniquely to deliver solutions that matter.

We work closely with refugee-supporting not-for-profits, settlement agencies, and affordable housing advocates, ensuring our impact reaches those who need it most — newcomers, refugees, Indigenous communities, and families facing hardship.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Our core values are woven into every partnership, every program, and every interaction.

Compassion

We approach every person with empathy, dignity, and respect. Everyone who walks through our doors deserves to feel welcomed and valued.

Community First

Our decisions are measured by the positive impact they create for families, individuals, and neighborhoods across Calgary.

Empowerment

We don't just provide help — we equip people with the skills, knowledge, and connections to thrive independently.

Inclusivity
